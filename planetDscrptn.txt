
*Ulnar- this is the planet that houses the government headquarters,
this is where you are asked to land in your assignment description. 
Also is the home of the Astromaterial Research (AMR) Laboratory.

*Zuria- This planet houses the simulation Supercomputing and Complex
Systems (SSCS) research laboratory.

*Poincare Belt- The main station, Ceta, is like the Earth city of 
Las Vegas with a wide array of travelers from all over. But this
area also happens to be a hotbed for criminals and underground
factions. 

*Oots - This is the moon of Zuria, it was once a bustling metropolis
with its own prestigious research center, but once that was destroyed,
it became a kind of ghost town.
